Any Questions

How does your cloud storage solution work for CCTV footage?
Our cloud storage solution provides:
  1. Secure upload of footage directly from your CCTV system to our cloud servers
  2. Unlimited storage capacity for long-term recording needs
  3. Encrypted data transmission and storage for maximum security
  4. Easy access to archived footage through our web portal or mobile app
What security services do you provide alongside cloud storage?
We offer comprehensive security services including 24/7 professional monitoring, rapid response teams, automated threat detection, and regular security assessments to ensure your property remains protected at all times.
How long can footage be stored in the cloud?
Our cloud storage solution allows for unlimited retention periods. You can store your footage for as long as needed, with options for 30 days, 90 days, 1 year, or custom retention periods based on your requirements.
What happens if there's an internet outage?
Our hardware includes local storage that temporarily saves footage during internet outages. Once connection is restored, the system automatically uploads the stored footage to the cloud, ensuring no gaps in your security coverage.
How secure is the cloud storage system?
We employ military-grade encryption for data transmission and storage, multi-factor authentication, regular security audits, and comply with all relevant data protection regulations to ensure your footage remains completely secure and private.
Can I access my footage remotely?
Yes, you can access your footage anytime, anywhere through our secure web portal or mobile app. View live feeds, playback recorded footage, and manage your security settings from any device with internet access.
